## Introduction

Schedules can be used to run any selection of your datasets on a schedule with granular control. That will make sure your data is up to date in your reports and dashboards or for other analysts to use.

<div className="bp3-callout bp3-icon-info-sign bp3-intent-warning" markdown="1">
Schedules <b>only run from the `production` branch</b>. Make sure you commit and push your changes to the production branch before editing your schedules.
</div>

## Use schedules

1. Navigate to your project's Scheduling page on the left menu bar
2. Click `Add schedule`
3. Fill the form and click `Create schedule`
